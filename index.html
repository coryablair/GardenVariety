Who we are

Scrappy
by
choice.

We're a small operation in Oldham County, not because we have to be, but because we believe the best farming happens at a scale where you can actually know your plants, your soil, and your customers.

We'll tell you what's in season and why, what failed this year and what surprised us, and which variety of carrot you should be roasting right now. Knowing where your food comes from should be easy. We try to make it that way.

Relational
We show up at market every week so you know who you're buying from. That matters to us.
Scrappy
We figure things out as we go, share what we learn, and don't pretend otherwise.
Educational
Ask us anything. We love talking about growing, cooking, and the why behind all of it.
